About MeetingMind AI

We're on a mission to make meetings productive again.

MeetingMind was born during the UK AI Agent Hackathon, powered by Z.AI's GLM models. We believe that every meeting should generate clear outcomes without extra effort.

Our intelligent agent integrates directly with your calendar, joins meetings, and uses state‑of‑the‑art language models to produce summaries, extract action items, and answer questions about past discussions. All while keeping your data secure and private.

Behind the scenes, we leverage Z.AI's GLM‑4 for reasoning and summarization, and GLM‑3‑turbo for fast transcription enhancement. The result is a production‑ready assistant that scales with your team.
